About the Nail Tech

Born and raised in Vancouver, Canada, Monica stands at a whopping 150cm. Her love of people developed through her experiences of working in the restaurant industry for more than 7 years. As a client herself, she always enjoyed getting her nails done. Like all human beings, she felt lost and distraught about where she wanted to go in life so she decided to merge the two: amazing nails and the human experience, by going to school in Japan to study the craft of Japanese nail art in Osaka.

Having studied the Japanese language since high school and having an interest in Japanese culture, moving to Japan was not a matter of “if” but “when”. The decision to study nails in Japan deemed the perfect opportunity to utilize all those skills learned in her previous years.

Monica believes that the perfect human experience starts by creating a healthy environment of safety for not only her clients, but her staff as well – whether they work directly with her, or they are simply delivering the paper outside. She believes in paying it forward by creating a circle of safety beyond our borders, regardless of status and/or rank.

Monica finds fulfillment in her work by creating a safe environment for everybody. Interacting with her clients and seeing their reactions throughout their journey - whether they are sitting with her at the nail table, or sitting at a table in the restaurants where she works at - brings her the utmost joy.

Work is such a huge aspect of life and she believes that feeling fulfilled at work is a human right and not a mere luxury. She wants to create a world where the majority of people wake up happy to go to work in the morning and come home feeling fulfilled by the work that they do. Through her work, she hopes to inspire you to pay it forward to create and maintain safe work environments so that you as well, can feel fulfilled by your work and inspire others that anything is possible when we feel like we belong, in any safe environment.
